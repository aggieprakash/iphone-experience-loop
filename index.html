Re-imagining the Product Life Cycle

The Hero's Journey provides a powerful framework for understanding how brands evolve from surface-level expression to meaningful customer transformation. This approach maps the entire lifecycle of product experience, revealing the critical moments where perception shifts and loyalty deepens.
